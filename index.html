STOP’s 4-Point Comprehensive Security Solution

STOP Security Plates give you piece of mind with a time-proven, multilayered anti-theft, recovery and inventory management system. Our serialized STOP Security Plates deter and protect your device from theft and provides for loss recovery. Our STOPMonitor software manages and remotely tracks devices while safeguarding your valuable data.

Prevent

Thieves don’t steal what they can’t sell.

Deter would-be thieves.

Our permanently bonded aluminum Security Plate is extremely difficult to remove, and the bold dissuasive message warns thieves the device is registered, monitored and virtually impossible to sell illegally.

Learn more

Protect

Devalued for criminal sale – permanently.

Protect from illicit resale.

If the rugged STOP Security Plate is forcefully removed, an indelible red “Stolen Property” tattoo is revealed, rendering the device useless to thieves by eliminating illicit resale value.

Learn more

Recovery

Get back what’s yours.

Recover your lost STOP-tagged devices.

STOP’s 24/7 Recovery Hotline and Secure Online Registration Database facilitate the return of lost, stolen and found devices to the rightful owners. As illustrated by our frequent recovery stories, the STOP system facilitates the return of lost devices. The result is a dramatic decrease in thefts and losses.

Learn more

Monitoring

Manage, track and safeguard your devices and data remotely.

STOPMonitor provides real-time asset management and device tracking functionality.

The STOPMonitor software provides an enterprise console for managing your fleet of assets. It also can geolocate the devices and remotely and reversibly encrypt and boot lock the hard drive.
